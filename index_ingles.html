Synopsis

The Shack

Mackenzie Phillips was physically and emotionally abused by his father when he was just 13 years old. As an adult, he apparently has a good life: three great kids, a beautiful wife, a lovely home... he doesn't seem to lack for anything.

One day Mack takes his kids camping, but what starts out as a fun getaway soon turns into every father's worst nightmare: his youngest daughter, Missy, is kidnapped from his camp and her body is never found. After a couple of nights, they find an abandoned cabin in the middle of the woods, where they find evidence of rape and murder. After that, Mack and his family are left with years of pain that they can't overcome.

As he struggles to make sense of such an enormous tragedy, Mack is plunged into a “great sadness”, his life begins to crumble and all the ghosts he thought buried long ago resurface to bring him to the edge of the abyss, his darkest hour has arrived.

Characters



MACK:
spiritual disciple

It represents the spiritual student or disciple. This is someone who knows the spiritual Path, but is going through a crisis of Faith, since the pain of the loss of his daughter has led him to disconnect from the Divine.



NAN
Advanced spiritual disciple

Mack is wife is an advanced spiritual student. She suffers the same loss as Mack, but that doesn't mean she loses her faith in God. Also for her, Dad expresses having a special affection for her.



KATE, JOSH Y MISSY
Mack Aspects

Missy means “bee honey”, spiritual food. She symbolizes trust in God, Purity and connection with the Divine. These are the aspects of himself that Mack loses and must regain through Forgiveness.



ADONAY (FATHER)
God Presence

God in the Father-Mother aspect (shown as a woman and called dad). It represents the I Am Presence.



JESÚS
Son of God Christ

God in the Son aspect. It symbolizes the Internal Christ.



SARAYU
Aspect Holy Spirit Life

God in the Holy Spirit aspect. It represents Life in form, the Creative Fire of Life. The name “Sarayu” means “common wind”, that is, Holy Breath. She collects the “tears” symbol of life experiences.



WISDOM
Ascended Master

She is an Ascended Master, related to the qualities of Wisdom and Truth, so she could be considered the Ascended Master Pallas Athena, Goddess of Truth and member of the Karmic Court. He receives Mack in a cave, a symbol of interiorization and deepening.
